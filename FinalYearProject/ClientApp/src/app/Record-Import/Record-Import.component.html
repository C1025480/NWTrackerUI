Record-Import

Instructions

  • In order to import records into the project you need to download this Template.
  • Once downloaded, fill in the columns of all the information required (e.g., house number, street name, and UPRN).
  • Save the document to a memorable location.
  • Click the Upload Button.
  • Browse to the saved location and upload the file to the webpage.
  • Click the Submit and Confirm Buttons.
  • Records will automatically be generated and placed into the commencement pending status.
  • If additional comments are required, find the record and edit it to add your comments.
